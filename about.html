An overnight success, 14-years in the making.

We started flipping houses in 2012, for 6 years we fell in love with renovating old homes. We learned more ripping old homes apart than we ever did from building new ones. After years of learning the trades we decided to give rental properties a chance, but we wanted to use our skills to create a great place at a fair price. Summer of 2019 we purchesed our first rental property and before we knew it, we had multiple properties, and residents. Every once in a while, we'd get an email from an investment firm. Their emails were confusing. They bragged about their AUM and IRR's and dropped that they'd gone to Harvard.

They were aliens. We did not speak their language. Spreadsheet jockeys who had never got their hands dirty.

Eventually, we gave in and sold one of our properties to them. It was a brutal six-month marathon. Piles of legal documents, constant renegotiations, and calls that made us feel uncomfortable. In the end we felt ripped off.

We want to be different.

We decided to build something better, using Warren Buffett and Charlie Mungers simple teachings. We worked backwards from everything we hated about selling, managment, and construction to build a process that is direct, respectful, and fast.

We make offers in days, not months, let owners dictate when they close (quick, slow, or something in between), and leave the decisions up to you.

Life is too short to sell to a guy who uses terms like "human capital".

Here's how we do it:

Speak plainly

Don't waste people's time. No buzzwords or finance theater.

Care

We only buy in areas we love and hold for the long run.

Close by

Our businesses work with eachother to offer the best outcomes

Make owners proud

We work hard to honor the original design and build on it.

No ego

Life is too short for brilliant jerks and narcissists.

Leave people alone

Buy great properties, put in great managment, and let them run.
